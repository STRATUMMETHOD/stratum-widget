The Four Requirements
01
Specific A concrete action — not a label or trait. "She volunteers" not "she's protective"
02
Visible A camera would capture it happening. "He examines every object" not "he feels curious"
03
Recurring A pattern across contexts, people, and time. Three instances minimum — not one moment
04
Consequential It shapes relationships, decisions, the arc. "Decorative" behavior won't pass this test

Stay in observer mode. What the camera sees — not what it means.
